 # ScrollWalker Privacy Policy

  **Last Updated:** November 24, 2025

  ## Overview
  ScrollWalker helps you balance screen time with physical activity by
  tracking your walking distance and app usage.

  ## Data Collection
  ScrollWalker collects and processes the following data:
  - **Health Data:** Step count and walking distance from Apple Health (with
   your permission)
  - **App Usage Data:** Time spent in apps via Screen Time API (with your
  permission)
  - **Local Storage:** All data is stored locally on your device

  ## Data Usage
  - Health and usage data is used solely to calculate your daily
  walk-to-scroll ratio
  - We do NOT share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties
  - We do NOT use your data for advertising
  - All data remains on your device

  ## Data Storage
  - All data is stored locally on your iPhone
  - We do not maintain servers or cloud storage
  - Data is never transmitted off your device

  ## Your Rights
  You can:
  - Revoke Health access anytime in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access &
  Devices
  - Revoke Screen Time access anytime in iOS Settings → Screen Time
  - Delete all app data by deleting the app

  ## Children's Privacy
  ScrollWalker is not intended for users under 13 years old.
